Almond Waffles 100% Keto

Almond Flour Waffles

This recipe is 100% Keto and it was change to fit my family needs, there is 4 of us and the original recipe was just for 2 waffles, this waffles are cripy and taste great.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Almond flour
  • 1 cup of Almond milk
  • 8oz of butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 8 tablespoon of Almond butter
  • 20grm of Stevia
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
